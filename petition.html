Graduate student mandatory fees petition

In the Fall 2019 semester, Greg Canal and I organized a petition to the Georgia Tech Administration regarding the mandatory fees charged to graduate students, which are the highest in the country.

We transmitted the petition to the administration in January 2020 with over 1000 signatures:
